What is KCETPredict?

KCETPredict is a free, student-built tool that helps engineering aspirants in Karnataka find eligible colleges using official KCET cutoff data. We parsed the KEA UGCET 2025 Round 2 Provisional Allotment Cutoff data — covering 226 colleges and 1,481 course entries — and built an easy-to-use search interface around it.

No logins. No fees. No hidden catches. Just honest, data-driven guidance to help you make one of the most important decisions of your academic career.

How to Use It

1
Get your KCET rank
Your rank will be available on the KEA portal (kea.kar.nic.in) after results are declared.
2
Select your category
Choose from GM, Category 1, 2A, 2B, 3A, 3B, SC, ST, or NRI depending on your reservation status.
3
Apply filters (optional)
Narrow by preferred branch (CSE, ECE, Mechanical, etc.) or city (Bangalore, Mysore, etc.)
4
Review your options
See all colleges where your rank falls within the cutoff. Results show cutoff ranks, your margin, and chance level.
5
Verify with KEA
Always confirm your shortlisted colleges on the official KEA portal before making any decisions.

Data Source

All cutoff data is sourced directly from:

Karnataka Examinations Authority (KEA)
UGCET-2025 Provisional Allotment Cutoff — Session 2 (Non-Interactive Admission System)
Generated: 29-August-2025

The data covers Rest of Karnataka seat types (not Bangalore Urban or other special zones unless part of the same document). Always check the full official document at kea.kar.nic.in.
