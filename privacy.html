1. Information We Collect

KCETPredict is designed to be privacy-friendly by default. The predictor tool runs entirely in your browser — we do not transmit your rank, category, or any inputs to our servers.

We may collect the following information automatically:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, operating system, and referring URL — collected anonymously via Google Analytics (if enabled).
  • IP Address: Automatically recorded by our web host (GitHub Pages) for traffic analysis.
  • Cookies: Set by third-party services like Google Analytics and Google AdSense (see below).

We do not collect: your name, email, phone number, KCET rank, category selection, or any personally identifiable information through the predictor tool itself.

2. How We Use Information

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Understand how many people visit the website and which pages are popular.
  • Improve tool performance and usability.
  • Display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics uses cookies to track anonymous visitor statistics.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant ads based on your browsing history across the web.
  • Functional cookies: We may use cookies to remember your preferences (e.g., last selected category) within a session.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some functionality. For more information on Google's use of cookies in advertising, visit Google's Advertising Policies.

4. Google AdSense & Advertising

KCETPredict uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google, as a third-party vendor, uses cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website and other websites on the internet.

Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to our site and/or other sites on the Internet. You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.

EU/UK Users: Google AdSense will show non-personalized ads by default in the EEA and UK. Users may be asked for consent where required by applicable law.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect data:

  • GitHub Pages — Hosting provider. May log IP addresses for security. GitHub Privacy Statement
  • Google Analytics — Anonymous traffic analysis. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Ad delivery and monetization. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google Fonts — Fonts are loaded from Google servers; Google may log your IP. Google Privacy Policy

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party services.

6. Children's Privacy

KCETPredict is intended for students aged 17 and above preparing for KCET (Karnataka Common Entrance Test). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt out of personalized advertising via Google's settings.
  • Right to Object: Object to how your data is processed.

Since the predictor itself collects no personal data, most of these rights apply only to analytics and advertising cookies. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below or adjust your browser/Google account settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically. Your continued use of KCETPredict after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
